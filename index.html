HEALTHY FAMILIES, HEALTHY COMMUNITIES

GBN India’s health sector programs are working to improve access for poor and marginalized communities to quality health services.Through identifying the root causes of healthcare problems, GBN India is working at patient, community and structural levels to establish innovative solutions and assist in the delivery of quality healthcare services. Cross-cutting approaches to improve the health and nutrition status of the poorest and excluded communities, particularly women and children, include partnering with community groups, work with government and civil society organizations.

EMPOWERING GIRLS THROUGH EDUCATION

GBN aims to improve the conditions under which girls, particularly in marginalized communities, can access quality education. Education is an important tool that allows women and girls to take part in decisions that affect their lives and improve their social status. It offers technical support through the formal school systems as well as through GBN India’s integrated programs. Education is one of the most effective agents of change in society. When a child is able to go to school today, he or she sets off a cycle of positive change.

ENSURING SUSTAINABLE LIVELIHOODS

GBN’s livelihood sector programs focus on women smallholders, entrepreneurs, employees and homeworkers from poor and marginalized communities, with the goal of empowering them with knowledge and enhancing access to inputs, services, technologies and opportunities. By engaging men and other influential actors, both private and public, involved in market value chains and entitlement delivery, we facilitate the building of an enabling and gender-transformative environment for conducting economic activities.

Our Missions

GBN has been working on economic development and social exclusion in India for many years. We do this through well-planned and comprehensive health, education, livelihoods, and preparedness and response to disasters programs. We also concentrate on information creation and sharing with various stakeholders to affect environmental effects on a scale. Our overall objective is to empower women and girls from poor and marginalized communities to improve their lives and livelihoods.
